About Us
Thai Central Mechanics Co., Ltd. (TCM1989) was established in 1989 as an industrial solutions provider specializing in the design, research and development, creation, and installation of prototype machinery for the manufacturing industry. Our services include raw material conveyor systems, automation systems, electrical and PLC control systems, factory structures and cranes, automated robotic arms, and the upgrading of existing machinery using modern technology. Our company has a skilled team of over 40 engineers and technicians with extensive experience. We continuously develop and improve our manufacturing processes to meet customer needs.
Thai Central Mechanics is a leader in material handling systems, production machinery, and automation systems. We offer consulting services in research, development, design, and construction of machinery for all types of manufacturing industries. With a team of experienced and highly skilled professionals, we currently design and build industrial machinery such as:
  • Design and Implementation of Turnkey Automation Systems: This includes plant layout design, production process sequencing, material handling systems, machinery in production lines, quality control systems, and packaging processes.
  • Installation and Supply of Robotic Systems: This includes designing and creating components for robotic arms (Grippers), programming robot operations, and designing and building support systems for robotic operations.
  • Electrical Systems for Factories: This includes designing and installing electrical systems for machinery, including PLC programming and controllers.
  • Design and Construction of Production Line Machinery: This covers all types of industries and manufacturing processes.
  • Upgrading Machinery and Production Lines: Enhancing machinery and production lines to improve efficiency and effectiveness.
  • Research and Development of Production Processes: Enhancing production efficiency, improving quality, and reducing waste.
Thai Central Mechanics Co., Ltd. has received various awards from activities and collaborations with different sectors, including government agencies, private organizations, and educational institutions. These awards are a testament to our commitment to developing products that meet high standards and quality, as well as our excellence in service recognized by leading customers.
