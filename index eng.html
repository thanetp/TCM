Thai Central Mechanics Co., Ltd. (TCM1989) was established in 1989 and specializes in providing industrial solutions, focusing on the design, research and development, construction, and installation of prototype machinery for the manufacturing industry.

Our customer base spans various industries, including electrical appliances, automotive and parts, construction materials, food and pharmaceuticals, and logistics. Thai Central Mechanics is a leader in conveyor systems, manufacturing machinery, and automation systems.

The goal of Thai Central Mechanics Co., Ltd. is to research and develop industrial machinery to help reduce costs for our customers, focusing on enhancing efficiency and sustainability in production processes through various approaches, including:

  • Production Process Efficiency: The company utilizes automation technology to enhance precision and reduce errors. This includes the implementation of sensor systems that detect hazards, thereby increasing safety in manufacturing.
  • Energy Efficiency: We focus on energy-saving solutions to lower costs and protect the environment. Our efforts include implementing renewable energy sources and energy-saving technologies.
  • Innovation and Development: We invest in new technologies and innovations that help reduce costs and meet market demands.
  • Environmental Responsibility: We are committed to reducing waste and promoting recycling. Our processes minimize waste generation and encourage the use of recyclable materials.

With these approaches, Thai Central Mechanics aims to be a leader in the machinery industry that is socially and environmentally responsible.
